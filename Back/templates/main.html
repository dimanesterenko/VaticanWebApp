About The Vatican Museum

The Vatican Museum is one of

The Vatican Museum is one of the best museum in the world

The Vatican Museum was founded by Pope
Julius II in the early 16th century.

The Vatican Museum preserves early Christian
relics from the 3rd to the 14th centuries.

It ranks third in the world for visitor numbers,
following only the Louvre and the Chinese
National Museum.

The museum houses about 70,000 works of art,
of which 20,000 are on public display.

Masterpieces of Art

The museum houses incredible works of art, including paintings by famous Renaissance masters, sculptures, mosaics and other artistic masterpieces.

Sistine Chapel

There is a unique opportunity to see Michelangelo's frescoes in the Sistine Chapel, including the famous Creation of Adam on the ceiling and the Last Judgment on the back wall.

Religious Relics

The Vatican Museum houses significant religious relics, including holy objects and artifacts associated with the papacy.

Online Booking

The possibility of convenient online ticket booking allows you to avoid queues and ensure a convenient visit schedule.

Special Events

Special exhibitions and tours are organized regularly, giving visitors the opportunity to discover new topics from different points of view.
